How can we help you?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
To create an account on ProTrade, click the 'Register' button in the top-right corner of our homepage. You'll need to provide your email, create a password, and complete a brief verification process. Once verified, you'll have immediate access to your new account dashboard.
What markets can I trade on?
ProTrade offers access to multiple markets including stocks, cryptocurrencies, forex, commodities, and indices. Our platform provides real-time data and advanced trading tools for all supported markets.
What are your trading fees?
Our fee structure is transparent and competitive. Stock trades start at $4.99 per trade with volume discounts available. Cryptocurrency trading fees range from 0.1% to 0.5% depending on volume. Forex and CFD spreads start from 0.8 pips. A complete fee schedule is available in the 'Fees' section of our website.
How secure is my data?
ProTrade implements bank-level security measures including 256-bit SSL encryption, two-factor authentication, and regular security audits. We never share your personal information with third parties except where required by regulations. Our systems are monitored 24/7 for unauthorized access attempts.
I forgot my password, what should I do?
To reset your password, click the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you instructions to create a new password. For security reasons, password reset links expire after 1 hour.
Do you have a mobile app?
Yes, ProTrade offers dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android devices. Our mobile platform includes all essential trading features of the desktop version, plus biometric authentication and push notifications for price alerts and trade executions.
Do you offer demo accounts?
Yes, we offer free demo accounts with $50,000 in virtual funds. Our demo environment mirrors real market conditions and includes all the features of our live trading platform. It's perfect for practicing strategies without financial risk.
How do I contact support?
Our support team is available 24/7 through multiple channels: Live chat on our website and app, Email at support@protrade.com, Phone at +1-800-PRO-TRADE, or via our support ticket system in your account dashboard.
